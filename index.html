About

Matthew Leitz is the founder of Leitz Partners. With experience spanning finance, human capital, operations, and growth, Matt partners with leaders of professional services firms to understand their goals and develop clear paths to accomplish them. He specializes in working with owner-operators considering expansion, serving as a bridge to a full-time leadership hire.

Matt is based in Brooklyn, New York, where he lives with his wife, daughter and dog. In the morning, you'll find him pushing a stroller to his favorite Cobble Hill coffee shops. In the evening, he's cheering on the Boston Celtics.
